What changed, and why

Five moves take it from “friendly app” to “trusted companion.”

01
Deeper, quieter colour

The bright magenta-purple is now a rich plum. It feels considered and clinical-grade rather than playful — better suited to a sensitive subject.

02
A serif voice

Spectral headings give the product warmth and gravity at once — the difference between an app and a companion.

03
Disciplined layout

One consistent grid, hairline dividers, lighter shadows and more breathing room. Less visual noise, clearer hierarchy.

04
Restrained accents

Green and red appear only where they carry meaning — victory and safety — so the SOS stays unmistakable.

05
Refined iconography

A single consistent line-icon set replaces mixed icon families for a coherent, premium feel.
